Theme Cafes

21. Howzatt

Howzatt at Gurgaon is a super cool pub and a brewery, frequented by the urban young Delhi crowd. Massive projector screens, glove-shaped seating and low lighting giving the perfect feel of sitting in a stadium and enjoying a live match is an absolute feast for a sports freak. Flags of different countries adorn the ceiling, giving the place a great sporty look.

22. Mumbai Matinee

Mumbai Matinee, Noida, is all about movies, actors/actresses and Bollywood drama. The 80’ theme, welcoming you with a red velvet curtain and great vinyl mirrors and pictures, posters of epic Bollywood movies and multi-cuisine that is set to uplift your mood with great hookah flavours also available.

23. 21 Gun Salute

21 Gun Salute at Gurgaon is a lavish retro dining spot that serves Indian and Nepalese dishes. Beautiful historical artefacts and murals on display take your breath away and delight you with the ambience showcasing the vintage car parked inside. Serving delicious food, the restaurant gives you a complete royal dining experience.

24. Chor Bizarre

As the name suggests Chor Bizarre, set in Daryaganj is all about vintage creativity. With a car turned into a buffet corner and various antiques donning the walls such as watches, lamps and pretty chairs, it looks like a thief market and serves multiple cuisines

25. The California Boulevard

The California Boulevard at Gurgaon is a stylish cafe known for its Hollywood theme and cuisines from 33 countries and the great variety of wines and cocktails that it serves. Old Hollywood movies play on the cinema screen, the bar at the cafe is based out on a tramcar, giving variety and entertainment for all the Hollywood fans.

26. Rodeo Connaught Place

Rodeo at Connaught Place is all about Tex-Mexican food, ambience, and great service. The Mexican wall art, murals, saddle stools at the bar, heavy wood usage, fake cactus plants and staff attired in the great sombrero hats are an added bonus to the delicious Mexican food served at the restaurant.

27. Yeti

Located in Greater Kailash 2, Yeti the Himalayan Kitchen boasts of authentic Nepalese and Tibetan cuisine, the motif being prominent in their ambience and theme. The restaurant is sure to take you on a reminiscent journey to the Himalayas.

28. Pirates of the Grill

This restaurant is known for its amazing barbeque, and grilled veg and non-veg delicacies and set out in a simple and informal style; it is based on a theme of private pirate’s ship along with brown and green colour. Situated in Rajouri Garden as well as Gurgaon, it offers a sumptuous buffet at great prices.

29. Jungle Jamboree

Jungle Jamboree has an ambience of a jungle and an aqua cave and also has an outdoor space, with the design and decor of a forest. Serving customers at Connaught Place and Gurgaon, it is a trendy restaurant, also known for being kid friendly as well.

30. Garam Dharam

One of the few of its kind, Garam Dharam restaurant is situated at Connaught Place Outer Circle, and as the name suggests, gives a great tribute to veteran actor Dharmendra. The desi décor gives a typical dhaba look, and the ambience is typically Desi Punjabi. A must-visit for Bollywood lovers, and desi food.
