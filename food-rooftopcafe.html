Rooftop Cafes

1. Potbelly Rooftop Cafe

Born out of all Delhiites’ love for trying novel experiences (especially in terms of food!), the Potbelly Rooftop Cafe is the most remarkable rooftop cafe in Delhi for the views, decor, ambience, comfort, and its unique Bihari cuisine with a twist! Dedicated to make your winter evenings a lot cosier and fun, Potbelly shouldn’t be given a miss if your tummy is asking for some delicious Litti Chokha!

2. Amalfi

If your soul is craving for some good, soothing music then head to Amalfi for some sweet tunes to feed to your ears. Though a little on the expensive side, Amalfi will make up for every penny spent with its wonderfully done decor, tranquil environment, stunning views from the sauve rooftop seating, friendly staff, and finger-licking cuisine. Located in suburbs of Greater Kailash 2, Amalfi is one of the best cafe in Delhi for couples.

3. Imperfecto

Overlooking the verdant greens of Hauz Khas village, Imperfecto is among the best offbeat cafes in South Delhi that attracts passersby with its unique looks and its offbeat sense of character and style. It’s the perfect dining spot for lovebirds as well as your gang of friends. Flaunting an earthly ambience with its wooden furnishings, exotic foreign cuisines, relaxed and serene set up, and an upstairs balcony overlooking the fort and greens of HKV, Imperfecto is the best rooftop cafe in Delhi for all who looking for much more than just good food.
