Malls

1. Ansal Plaza

Ansal Plaza is part of a massive complex - HUDCO Place, built on 35 acres of land and remains one of the most monumental and prestigious achievements of Delhi till date. HUDCO Place is situated at a very strategic location, just half a kilometer from south Delhi's major commercial hub, South Extension - in the very midst of posh colonies.

Location: HUDCO Place, Khelgaon Marg, New Delhi

2. City Square mall

City Square Mall is a medium sized mall in Rajouri Garden. Located in Shivaji District Centre, close to the metro station, City Square Mall was developed by the MGF Group. The main feature of this mall is its large lifestyle department store. There are branded stores at the mall like Nike and KFC.

Location: Shivaji Place Tagore Garden Extension,New Delhi

3.Cross River Mall

Located in Shahdara, Cross River Mall is one of the most famous shopping destinations in Delhi. This multi-storey mall is a premier hub when it comes to brand shopping and a casual meeting with friends in between your retail therapy as there are a number of cafes, restaurants and movie halls inside the mall. The mall area, spread across an area of around 12, 380 meters, is done with high quality marbles and reflective glasses and is quite a sight to look at.

Location: Shahdara, New Delhi

4. DLF Emporio

All stores, sales, events, restaurants, brands at DLF Emporio. ‘Emporio’ has been designed by architect Mohit Gujral as a premium luxury shopping-cum-recreation center. Since all spaces are to be leased only, the ‘Emporio’ management will have complete control of the tenant mix - thereby ensuring the highest levels of exclusivity for all its lessees. Other planned attractions include fine dining restaurants, a spa, a salon and a member’s club; all designed to invite people to spend an entire day in five-star luxury.

Location: Vasant Kunj, New Delhi

5.Metro Walk Mall

Metro Walk was developed by the Appughar Group[1] and the Unitech Group. It is located in Sector 10 of Rohini, adjacent to Rithala Metro Station. The mall and amusement park were master planned by the Florida-based company Bose International. It has an 220,000 sq ft (20,000 m2) outdoor shopping area which houses outlets of many leading national and international brands including Pantaloons, Reebok, and Nike

Location: Rohini, New Delhi

6. North Square Mall

North Square Mall is located at Pitampura.This first of its kind woman's mall offers a lot of ambience, panache, and ease of shopping which every woman wants very much. This mall provides a very good parking facility to the shoppers as well as a 3 screen multiplex and a play area for children. It is very well constructed and the interiors of the mall are very impressive. It is very well maintained, the washrooms are kept clean. It provides a good parking facility to its customers.

Location: Pitampura, New Delhi

7. Select Citywalk

Select CITYWALK is located in the Saket District Centre, in one of the upcoming middle-class neighbourhoods of Saket in south Delhi. It was the first large-scale shopping center to open in South Delhi and was the first of four major malls that were planned for the Saket District Centre. The other neighbouring malls include the MGF Metropolitan Mall and the DLF Place.

Location: Saket, New Delhi

8. Shop In Park North

Situated in vicinity of North Delhi?s posh localities like Ashok Vihar, Model Town, Pitampura, Rohini, Adarsh Nagar and Shalimar Bagh, Shop-in Park North brings with it boundless business potential and opportunities. Empowered with high disposable income, the population residing in these localities gives assured footfalls and a gravity defying revenue curve.

Location: Shalimar Bagh, New Delhi

9. TDI Mall

TDI Mall is a part of the Shivaji Place District Centre which is being planned in consultation with internationally renowned real estates consultants Jone Lang Lassalle Megraj, comprising of shopping centers, commercial and office buildings, five-star hotel and a Habitat Center on the lines of India Habitat Center.

Location: Rajouri Garden, New Delhi

10. TDI Paragon Shivaji Place District Centre

TDI Paragon has been created as a ground-plus-two structure with two level basements for parking and services. The third floor is dedicated entirely to entertainment with a four screen multiplex enclosing a huge concession area. The stunning use of vibrant colours and glass on the plaza facades provide the mall with a highly attractive and modernistic appeal.

Location: Rajouri Garden, New Delhi
