Cheap Cafes

1. Big Yellow Door

You’ll know it when you see it! The asymmetrical, crooked yellow door that leads you into this café smelling deliciously of herbs and coffee makes it one of the most fun places in Delhi! The hotspot of all college going kids in Delhi, this is one of the most pocket friendly cafes in Delhi that has become the statement landmark of the Satya Niketan area. The best part – it serves the most delicious food in the lowest of prices amid warm interiors and great music (unlike those shady restaurants).

2. The Hudson Cafe

The most popular of affordable cafes in Delhi, The Hudson Cafe is a big hit among college students, school kids, and everyone else because of its magnificent Oreo Shakes and Red Velvet Cakes. While the amazing and quality food will go incredibly easy on your pocket, the cafe’s looks will blow your mind! An interesting decor with ornate mirrors on the ceiling and captivating wall art, Hudson Cafe’s beautifully done interiors are quite the catch that draw people here. Don’t leave without trying their Lasagna and Gemini Pizza.

3. Cafeteria & Co.

Don’t go by their looks. This cafe will go as easy on your wallet as appealing it is to your eyes and taste buds. Cafeteria and Co. is known as one of the best cheap cafes in Delhi thanks to their warm vibes, inviting ambience, superb food, star shaped burgers, Hexza Pizzas, Tiramisu Shakes, Beyonce Burgers, Peri Peri Chicken….well, you got the point! It’s the best place for the broke and hungry.
