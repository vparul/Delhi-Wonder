14. DLF Mall of India

DLF Mall of India is the largest mall in India with a retail space of 2 million square feet (GLA). Strategically divided into 5 zones that are spread over 7 floors it is resplendent with fabulous fashion stores, delectable dining options and energizing entertainment arenas. It houses 330 brands that include 100 Fashion brands, along with 75 Food & Beverages options, and countless entertainment activities. Not only does it deliver a rewarding shopping exposure, but also an exhilarating entertainment and a sensational dining experience like never-before, making it the first Destination Mall of the Country.

Location: Sector 18,Noida

15.Sab Mall

Sab Mall: A new addition to the shoppers haven of Atta Market, Sab is a one-stop lifestyle mall. Brands include Ashima-Leena, Allen Solly and Wills Lifestyle, to name a few. Quikys Coffee is nice for a hot cuppa or you can try Orrista for snacks too.
