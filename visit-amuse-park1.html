Amusement Parks

11. Delhi Eye

A 45-metre gigantic Ferris wheel is set atop a public garden called KalindiKunj Park. Based on the similar but older London Eye and Singapore Flyer, the Ferris wheel comprises of 36 air-conditioned cabins. With each rotation taking 20 minutes, Delhi’s iconic monuments of QutbMinar, Red Fort, the Baha’I Temple, and the Humayun’s Tomb dot the panoramic scenery that changes with every passing minute it's an amazing place to visit in Delhi. To capture the magnanimity of Delhi atop everything, head to Delhi Eye.

Location: Kalka Mandir Metro Station

12.Delhi Rides Amusement Park

One the most well-known amusement parks in Delhi, Delhi Rides is attached to the Delhi Eye. A range of whirling rides thrill adults, while the kids have dedicated rides for a day of frolicking fun.Delhi Rides is an ideal destination to spend time with your dear ones, and the tangy fast food served within the premises is great way to unwind after a day filled with joyrides. A small water park that is attached is one of Delhi’s favourite ways to beat summer.

Location: Kalka Mandir Metro Station.

13. Scary House

Enter the netherworld as you wobble through Delhi’s Scary House, with dangling skeletons and eerie screams keeping you on your toes throughout. The out-of-the-world concepts are a result of some brilliant animatronics, which use robotic props to scare the living daylights out of you.Vivid visuals of the half-dead jumping right at you leaves one gasping for breath—even as the kids are experiencing the scare of their lives! Visit with family to enjoy a day filled with gasps and shrieks.

Location: Rajouri Garden

14. Kingdom of Dreams

Vouched as the country’s first live entertainment, theatre and leisure destination, Kingdom of Dreams offers the best India has to offer. The cuisines, carnivals, mythological shows, street dances, crafts offer you the ultimate Indian experience. The extravaganza of Indian cinema comes alive inside the NautankiMahal, where the stage is set alight by world-class productions.

Location: IFFCO Chowk (Yellow Line)

15.Hangout

Hangout is a jungle-themed Family Entertainment Centre, tailor-made for kids. The Kiddie Area teams with a rock-climbing wall, virtual rollercoasters, sky-tube and many more attractions on its three-level playground. At the heart of Hangout is a flurry of amusement rides like the Tea-Cup and the Pirate Boat.

Location: Malviya Nagar
