Book Cafes

1. Cha Bar

Library cum café, Cha Bar in CP is among the best cafes in Delhi for bookworms where you can grab a book, pick a comfortable spot, and binge your favorite food. The fascinating interiors compliment the nerdy aura with a simple white and blue decoration and a quiet atmosphere that is essential for reading. One will find here books of every kind ranging from history to fantasy to biography. They have around 60 different kinds of teas, ice creams, and tantalising food items including Bun Kebabs, Fish n Chips, Chicken Shawarma Wrap among others , making Cha Bar the best of all-rounder book cafes in Delhi.

2. Rico’s

Apart from having a fancy collection of books and fabulous Italian dishes for your appetite, this place is also one of the best coffee shops in Delhi. Best books, and best coffee – excellent combination, don’t you think? The costliest item on their menu is of mere INR 200/-, making this place a great option for youngsters and college kids. Don’t forget to try the awesome Chilli Cheese Fingers offered here at one of the most affordable cafes in Delhi.

3. Ivy & Bean

No matter how fast-paced life is, the busy beavers of Delhi always find time to visit this quaint cafe where they can enjoy some peace time with their favourite book and a cuppa hot coffee. Their funda of ‘The Book Of The Week’ is a hit among Delhi’s young, literature-lover population, just as are their range of mouth-watering snacks. Not just readers, Ivy & Bean is also one of the best book cafes in Delhi for working professionals and freelancers who can use this quiet space to work, courtesy the high-range WiFi.
