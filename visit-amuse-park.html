Amusement Parks

1. Worlds of Wonder

One of the largest amusement parks in Asia, Worlds of Wonder is located at Sector 38A Noida and has about 20 rides. Attached alongside the world-class amusement park is a water park with plenty of rides of its own.Worlds of Wonder is divided into two zones called the Teen Zone and a family zone named La Fiesta. Rapid and Rip Curl, Mega Disco and Rocking Roller are only a few of the hair-raising rides housed inside this fantasy land.

Location: Noida Sector 18

2.Drizzling Land

Calling itself an aquatic adventure park, Drizzling Land on the Delhi-Meerut road has a Disk Jockey on-premise dishing out groovy tunes. This water park in Delhi-NCR has a few thrilling rides catering to all age groups. While Revolving Tower, Wave Pool, Disc Coaster, and many more rides enthral the adults, a special Kids zone offers children unforgettably adventurous rides.

Location: 8th Mile Stone, Delhi-Meerut Expressway

3. Adventure Island

Situated within the premises of a Mall, Adventure Island encompasses 25 vertigo-inducing rides that will leave you lightheaded—and still begging for more. One of the most frequented amusement parks in Delhi, Adventure Island also offers great food, entertainment and a noteworthy shopping experience thus, making it must do activity in Delhi.Spread across more than 60 acres, Adventure Island’s Freefall rides, Demolition Derby, Twister and Flip-Out are a few popular attractions inside this amusement park in New Delhi.

Location: Rithala Metro Station, Sector 10, Rohini

4. Aapoo Ghar

Among the earliest amusement parks in New Delhi, it is also perhaps the most loved. An ideal weekend getaway from Delhi, Aapno Ghar is safely thrown open for all age groups. The ever-popular rides of Baby Train, Caterpillar, Mono-cycles, and Jet Plane delight the kiddies.Aapno Ghar blends lush greens with its water pools and slides, while its video game parlour is an unmissable attraction. The Family Slides and Rain Dance at this amusement park in Gurgaon can turn out to be hilarious experiences with the family.

Location: NH8, Gurgaon

5.Fun N Food Village

A 400-feet long water channel called the Lazy River beckons you to cool off under the insolent Delhi sun. With a total collection of 21 amusement rides and 22 water slides, it is among the oldest amusement parks in Delhi-NCR.Located in the midst of placid surroundings, Fun N Food Village is home to the largest wave pool in Delhi. Oh yes, Fun N Food Village is all about that—delicious food and lots of fun!

Location: Old Delhi Gurgaon Road, Kapashera

6. Just Chill Water park

One of the newest amusement parks in Delhi, Just Chill comprises the exact ingredients to while your cares away on any given weekend. The insane Black Thrill takes you on an adrenaline-surging journey through a completely enclosed tube, while other attractions such as the Rainbow Godzilla and Katrina Twist live up to their snazzy names. A variety of adventure games at Just Chill are wonderful means to unreel.

Location:Main GT Karnal Road, near GTB Memorial

7. Splash

Interestingly situated well within the confines of natural beauty, Splash houses a mix of water and amusement rides. Cyclone Family Slide, Mushroom Fall, Harakiri and Multilane slides entice you to experience a blast of adrenaline.While amusement rides like the Mini Columbus, Striking Car and the mini Ferris wheel are jovial enough to fascinate kids. For the quintessential joy rides with family, head to Splash and forget how old you are.

Location: Jahangirpuri (Yellow Line)

8. Wet N Wild

This water park in Gurgaon is the first of its kind in the country. A conglomeration of offerings such as the water park, discotheque, spa, and a line-up of restaurants make Wet N Wild one of the most preferred amusement parks in Delhi-NCR.The exclusive water park with its array of Tornado, Pendulum and a host of cold water pools, Wet N Wild is best explored as a full two-day trip to experience wholesome thrills.

Location: Sector 78, NH8, Gurgaon

9. Jurasic Park Inn

As the name suggests, this amusement park in Delhi-NCR has a T-Rex standing guard at the entrance. Other 30-odd life-size dinosaurs like the Carnotaurus and the Velociraptor are found everywhere inside the park—ones that even growl and move their heads!The amusement-cum-water park is one of the picnic spots near Delhi and provides accommodation facilities as well. 21 rides and six dizzying slides, and a special zone for hosting events. Don’t miss.

Location: GT-Karnal Road, NH1, Near Omaxe City

10. Entertainment City

Among the latest attractions in the city, Entertainment City or E-City is spread across a sprawling 145 acres! The looping rollercoaster, and many of the 30 rides which are no less than adventure sports in Delhi are sourced from the same manufacturers of rides at Universal Studios and Disneyland.

Location: FC-7, Film City, Sector 16A, Noida
